The Five Rings

Five distinct civilizational environments — not a hierarchy of suffering, but a hierarchy of institutional investment. Each layer is complete on its own terms.

As institutional presence withdraws with descent, private enterprise and organic social order expand to fill the space. The higher the layer, the more VMSS maintains on your behalf. The lower the layer, the more that space is filled by private institutions and individual capability. Cross-layer visitation flows downward — citizens may visit layers below their placement, arriving economically neutral in the destination environment.

Heaven Ring · Pre-Intervention

+1 Sanctuary

Informal name: The Heaven Layer

STI threshold
85–100

The highest-trust environment. Harm is stopped before it completes — the Threshold Inhibition Protocol halts acts mid-execution via neural inhibition and drone countermeasures. Residents have earned placement through sustained verified non-harmful conduct. The result is an environment defined not by the absence of freedom, but by the absence of threat.

Economy

$10,000/month UBI + Primary Job Subsidy. Full post-scarcity baseline. Shared currency with Main Layer. Stock speculation excluded.

Enforcement

Pre-intervention. Harmful acts cannot complete. Perpetrators reassigned immediately on attempt.

Access

Selective Ascension Domains (SADs) — opt-in purity zones each gated by a single measurable metric. Full augmentation and longevity access.

The Proving Ground · Post-Intervention

Main Layer (0)

STI threshold
70–84

Where every citizen begins. The largest ring by population — the density, energy, and variety of a civilization at full human scale. Harmful acts can complete before intervention, which is what makes the choices here real. Full economic support, full personal autonomy, and full access to VMSS technologies. This is where moral character is demonstrated, not assumed.

Economy

$10,000/month UBI + Primary Job Subsidy. Shared currency with Sanctuary. Highly socialised, post-scarcity.

Enforcement

Post-intervention. Acts may complete. Victims restored via backup vessel. Perpetrators reassigned immediately.

Mobility

Upward to Sanctuary via sustained compliance. Downward via breach or voluntary choice. All movement is permanent for punitive descent.

Civic Violation Tier · Post-Intervention

-1 Noncompliance

Informal name: The Balanced Layer

STI threshold
50–69

The first layer of consequence — for chronic low-harm violations, fraud, harassment, and repeated antisocial conduct that falls short of predatory harm. Life remains materially stable. What changes is the texture: reduced UBI, thinner institutional coverage, and a contracted social trust environment. Punitive reassignment is permanent. The layer is a consequence state, not a failure state.

Economy

$5,000/month UBI + Primary Job Subsidy. Hybrid public/private. Own siloed currency. 45–50% tax on income >$10M.

Enforcement

Post-intervention. Logging only, no preemptive protection. Revival serviced via VMSS satellite installation.

Placement

Punitive reassignment is permanent. STI improvement determines local standing and access within the layer, not return to a higher layer.

Severe Harm Tier · Post-Intervention

-2 Violent Offense

Informal name: The Lower Restrictions Layer

STI threshold
30–49

For citizens who have committed serious predatory harm — rape, severe assault, systematic coercive violence. The institutional safety net has substantially withdrawn. Private enterprise fills the void. Victims are still restored where infrastructure remains. Punitive reassignment here is permanent. Life, commerce, and genuine social order continue on the layer's own terms.

Economy

$2,500/month UBI + Primary Job Subsidy. Predominantly private. Own siloed currency. 20–25% tax on income >$10M.

Enforcement

Post-intervention, logging only. Murder triggers immediate -3 reassignment. Revival via VMSS satellite installation.

Social order

Private security, territorial hierarchy, organic community. Public ledger follows every resident — the voluntary population builds a premium environment distinct from the punitive.

Capital Offense Layer · Minimal Institutional Presence

-3 Terminal

Informal name: The Freedom Layer

STI threshold
0–29

The final ring. Minimal VMSS institutional presence — a federal floor of UBI, taxation, and federal law enforcement, with daily governance withdrawn. No revival infrastructure. Death is final — the implant severs the backup vessel link programmatically at terminal reassignment, enforced at the hardware level. A meaningful portion of -3 residents chose to be there — voluntary libertarian residents who prefer maximum freedom over institutional protection. The layer stratifies organically. What VMSS doesn't design, human social physics produces on its own.

Economy

$1,250/month UBI. Earth-like capitalism — equity markets, speculation, private banking. 10–15% tax on income >$10M. Own siloed currency.

Enforcement

None from VMSS. Private institutions fill the void. The public ledger travels with every resident — private communities use it to make their own decisions.

Continuity

Severed at arrival. No VMSS fabrication satellite present. Death is final without exception. Bailout options unavailable.
