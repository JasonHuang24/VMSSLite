Core Technologies

The technological foundation that makes moral causality operational rather than rhetorical.

Technoneural Implants

The central nervous system of VMSS. Voluntary brain-computer interfaces implanted at entry. Serve as identity anchor, intent monitor, failsafe device, personal AR dashboard, and backup vessel sync point. Thoughts remain completely private — only outward expression and executed acts affect STI or enforcement thresholds.

  • Neural pattern scanning — distinguishes intent from thought
  • Real-time intent and execution detection with warnings
  • User-configurable failsafe motor inhibition
  • AR overlays and personal dashboards
  • STI ledger integration — two-track: social trust and criminal record
  • Backup vessel sync and continuity anchoring
  • International passport — the implant serves as identity, citizenship proof, and behavioural record during international travel

Backup Vessels

Periodic encrypted mind-state backups stored in secure vaults. Upon death, the latest backup transfers to a pre-grown vessel. Preserves continuity of identity — not moral status. Revival is binary: full fidelity or revival failure (death). There is no partial reconstruction, no degraded copy, no "close enough."

Fabrication is sovereign VMSS technology manufactured exclusively in Main Layer and Sanctuary. In -1 and -2, continuity is serviced through VMSS-operated fabrication satellite installations — closed, sovereign facilities inaccessible to the local economy. Elevated revival failure probability reflects reduced infrastructure: +1 Sanctuary & Main ~1 in 1,000,000; -1 Noncompliance ~1 in 10,000; -2 Violent Offense ~1 in 1,000. In -3, the implant severs the backup vessel link programmatically at terminal reassignment. Death in -3 is final, enforced at the hardware level.

"Backup vessels preserve continuity, not innocence."

Neural Diving

Direct consensual mind-to-mind interface. Audience Mode allows passive observation of another person's subjective experience. Pilot Mode allows temporary active inhabitation with fully revocable consent. Used for education, therapy, empathy training, creative collaboration, and augmentation previews. All sessions logged for safety. Non-consensual content triggers immediate reassignment.

Enforcement Systems

Medical drones are the rapid response layer of a fully integrated medical system — not ambulances, but the instrument that closes the gap between incident and hospital-grade care to near-zero. Deployed instantly after any harm event in Main and above. Nanite injectors stabilise and treat in the field; severe cases are transported to institutional hospital infrastructure. In Heaven Layers, drones also serve preventive and chronic care functions, integrating with hospitals for continuous monitoring without scheduling friction.

Enforcement turrets and drone swarms handle pre-intervention in Heaven (halting acts mid-motion) and post-intervention logging and evidence capture in Main and below.

AR surveillance — drone patrols, security cameras, citizen implants, and government-grade biometric cameras — makes identity non-repudiable regardless of implant status. Removing an implant does not erase your record.

Medical completeness is a named leakage category — the aspiration that every known physical ailment is treatable for every resident within institutional reach. In upper layers this is a technology problem that closes as science advances (current delivery ~35–40%). In lower layers it is an access problem: the cure may exist civilizationally but private market dynamics determine who reaches it. The terminal layer's medical leakage floor is structural rather than technological.

Military Technologies — Classified

The military capability of a civilization at VMSS's technological level does not resemble conventional warfare. Two primary instruments are publicly acknowledged. Operational specifics are classified.

Implant kill switch — hardware-level, accessible only through national military command authority. Instantaneous, simultaneous at any scale. No collateral damage, no emissions. This capability is domestic only — it operates on residents who consented to implant installation and has no external application.

Nanobot neutralisation plumes — classified technology for non-implanted threats. Intelligent targeting, near-instant lethality, minimal emissions, each capsule capable of neutralising thousands. Closes the primary evasion vector: removing an implant to evade the kill switch enters this instrument's operational envelope instead. Unlike the kill switch, nanobot plumes do not require implant consent and constitute the civilisation's primary external deterrent.

Biological Augmentation

Consensual, reversible modifications using nanotech, gene editing, and organoid fabrication. Practical lifespan of 200–300 years, theoretical upper limit approaching 1,000. Fertility window extends to 500 years. Age pinning, transrace, transanimal traits, and custom modifications available. Subsidised in higher layers. Previews available via neural diving.

Mega-Walls & Boundary Infrastructure

Continuous boundary walls separating the five rings: 15km above ground, 5km below ground, 100m thick. The above-ground height clears commercial aviation altitude entirely. The sub-surface depth closes tunnelling approaches. Seismic sensors, ground-penetrating radar, persistent drone swarms, and automated turrets are integrated throughout.

Forcefield integration is planned for the 28th century as Dyson-class energy infrastructure matures — adding an energy barrier layer on top of the physical structure. By 2850, the boundary is impenetrable by any means available to individuals or organised groups.

Automated Labor & UBI Foundation

AI-driven automation generates the surplus that funds Universal Basic Income across all layers. In Heaven and Main, AI handles 90%+ of production. In lower layers, automation infrastructure is thinner — creating genuine demand for human labour and a rawer market economy where capability commands direct value. The Primary Job Subsidy steers labour toward critical infrastructure roles without coercion.
