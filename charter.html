The Charter

The binding constitutional form of The Five Rings. For the expanded rationale and institutional logic, read the Whitepaper.

Preamble

We establish The Five Rings Civilization as a voluntary society governed by immutable moral causality. Harm is structurally mitigated or segregated. Abundance is the birthright of compliance. Technology liberates the self while preserving the innocent. Every citizen begins with full dignity; consequences follow choices without mercy or malice.

Article I – Vertical Moral Stratification System

Layers are sovereign strata of reality, assigned by demonstrated intent and execution of harm. Heaven Layers (+1 and SADs): Pre-Intervention — acts halted mid-execution. Main Layer (0): Post-Intervention — acts may complete; victims revived/treated, perpetrators reassigned. Lower Layers (-1, -2): Post-Intervention with partial institutional presence. -3 Terminal: Zero intervention.

Article II – Social Trust Index (STI)

Separate reputational ledger for non-criminal trust violations. Tiered visibility: minor private, major public. Gates Trust Purity Domains. No crossover to VMSS reassignment. Dips only on outwardly expressed actions — no thought penalties or gains.

Article III – Economic Framework

III.I – UBI: $10,000/month (+1/Main) → $5,000 (-1) → $2,500 (-2) → $1,250 (-3). No layer permits starvation. Indexed to 2025 Earth values.

III.II – Primary Job Subsidy: Matching UBI payment for one qualifying job of 20+ hours/week of critical infrastructure work. A time dividend — 20 hours unlocks the subsidy, freeing the rest of the week.

III.III – Taxation: 90–99% (+1/Main) → 45–50% (-1) → 20–25% (-2) → 10–15% (-3) on annual income exceeding $10,000,000. Proportional to institutional benefits received.

III.IV – Currency Siloing: +1 and Main share a currency. -1, -2, -3 each maintain separate non-convertible currencies. Visitors arrive economically neutral.

III.V – Asset Liquidation: Involuntary descent: 100% to treasury. Voluntary descent: progressive retention (1–10% depending on net worth). Pre-positioning within 24 months prior to descent is subject to the same schedule.

III.VI – Upward Mobility: Voluntary descent does not permanently close upward eligibility. Punitive reassignment to -2 and -3 closes the pathway permanently. -1 preserves rehabilitation pathways.

III.VII – Savings Circulation Mandate: Tiered across all layers. +1/Main: 10% monthly garnishing at $100k average trigger. -1: 5% at $50k average. -2/-3: 5% on UBI-origin savings only, at $25M and $10M aggregate thresholds. All garnished funds return to the Automation Dividend Treasury as UBI. Population denominator by registered home address.

III.VIII – Terminal Realm Economic Autonomy: -3 Terminal operates outside VMSS economic oversight entirely. Earth-like capitalism — equity markets, speculation, private banking — operates freely. Currency siloing prevents destabilisation of upper layer economics.

Article IV – Technology & Continuity

Implants, neural diving, biological augmentation, backup vessels (0.5–2% drift risk), enforcement protocol (pre in Heaven, post in Main/below).

Backup vessel fabrication is sovereign VMSS technology manufactured in Main Layer and +1 Sanctuary only. In -1 and -2, continuity is serviced through VMSS-operated fabrication satellite installations. In -3, the implant severs the backup vessel link programmatically at terminal reassignment — enforced at the hardware level. Death in -3 is final without exception.

Article V – Rights & Stances

Freedom of speech (unless harm threshold crossed). Consensual adult prostitution permitted. All body transmutations permitted. Abortion illegal — fetus reincarnated via backup vessel in Main Layer autoparenting with neutral status. Children born in lower layers remain with parents; the right to relocate to Main Layer (0) is a standing human right exercisable at any age, enforceable without parental consent.

Article VI – Enforcement & Restoration

Heaven (+1 and SADs): Pre-Intervention — no harm completes. Main (0) and -1/-2: Post-Intervention — acts may complete, victims restored, perpetrators reassigned. -3 Terminal: Zero-Intervention — no restoration, no protection.

Article VII – Layer Mobility, Visitation & Ascension

Upward movement earned through sustained compliance. Punitive reassignment to -2 and -3 is permanent. -1 preserves rehabilitation pathways. Visitation flows downward only. Voluntary -3 residents retain full downward visitation rights. The layers are not a hierarchy of suffering — they are distinct civilizational environments each complete on its own terms.

Article VIII – Child Protection & Autoparenting

Every fetus is automatically linked to a backup vessel at the moment of detected pregnancy. If the pregnancy is terminated, the fetus is reincarnated in Main Layer autoparenting. Children born in lower layers remain with their parents. The right to relocate to Main Layer (0) is a standing human right at any age, enforceable without parental consent. Every child has an independent AI legal advocate from birth.

Articles IX–XX — Governance Principles

Selective Ascension Domains (SADs) — voluntary revocable purity zones (IX). Exit always permitted from Main and above, re-entry requires moral accounting (X). Amendment by unanimous Heaven + supermajority Main only; core principles immutable (XI). Non-deterministic evaluation — no single metric determines layer assignment (XII). Signal/decision separation — STI informs but does not dictate (XIII). Proportional response (XIV). Rehabilitation pathways preserved in all non-terminal layers (XV). System stability over short-term reactivity (XVI). Resistance to exploitation (XVII). Contextual evaluation (XVIII). Feedback loop awareness (XIX). System accountability (XX).

Article XXI – Zero Leakage Aspiration

The founding aspiration of The Five Rings is 0% leakage across all categories. This is not a promise — it is a direction. The civilization measures itself against perfection rather than against historical precedent. Every percentage point of improvement is a civilizational achievement. Target trajectory: ~90% leakage at founding (2026) → ~25% by 2150 → ~0.01% by 3000.

"The Five Rings Civilization does not force virtue. It structures reality so that harm self-isolates, trust becomes legible, and continuity becomes a contract — not an escape."
