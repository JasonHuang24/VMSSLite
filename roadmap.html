Development Roadmap

Seven phases across 974 years. From the 2026 Boise Accord to a civilization approaching the theoretical limits of consequence delivery by 3000. Starting leakage: ~90%. Target: 0.01%.

21st Century — Where We Actually Are

Using a weighted assessment across all technology categories, VMSS today delivers approximately 10% of its stated promise — operating at approximately 90% leakage. Three categories are load-bearing with veto weight: backup vessels (~0% delivery), the implant ledger (~10%), and autonomous enforcement (~5%). The headline figure is dragged to near-total by these three foundational gaps — progress in supporting systems doesn't compensate for their absence.

The conceptual and institutional foundations are further along than the leakage figure suggests. The gap is engineering, not imagination. We are drafting blueprints for an aircraft in an era without aluminium, combustion engines, or aerodynamic theory. The blueprint is sound. The world needs time.

Foundation & Research
2026 – 2070 · ~90% leakage
Boise Accord signed. Sovereign charter entity established. Core team assembled across neuroscience, AI, materials engineering, ethics, and law. First whitepaper and charter released. Brain-computer interface research begins — early implant prototypes in animal models, then limited human trials. Backup vessel research initiated as a long-horizon programme. No physical ring construction. The 21st century ends with the framework established and the first technologies beginning to approach — but not yet crossing — their critical thresholds.

Technology Threshold Crossing & First Prototype
2070 – 2150 · ~90% → ~40–50% leakage
Backup vessel technology achieves first reliable human revival — the most significant technological event in VMSS history. Neural implant ledger achieves continuous identity-anchored monitoring at small scale. Autonomous enforcement crosses civilian deployment threshold. First small prototype community established — hundreds to low thousands of voluntary citizens operating under full VMSS conditions. Full UBI and subsidy systems operational within the prototype. Leakage within the prototype estimated at 40–50%.

Planetary Consolidation
2150 – 2350 · ~25% → ~8% leakage
Full planetary population across all rings reaches billions. Wall construction phases begin. Orbital habitats and first permanent off-world outposts established. Lifespan extension reaches 250–300+ years. VMSS becomes the dominant governance model for new human settlements. Early interstellar probe missions launched with backup vessel technology aboard.
3
Infrastructure Hardening
2350 – 2500 · ~8% → ~1.5% leakage
Full wall network construction across all five ring boundaries. Sub-surface foundations proven to eliminate tunnelling. Backup vessel drift risk declining toward sub-0.5%. Enforcement drone coverage expanding toward full saturation. AI governance systems accumulate two centuries of edge case data. Lower layer private economies mature — -3 sub-stratification into voluntary and punitive communities well-established. First Dyson swarm feasibility studies completed.
4
Systemic Refinement
2500 – 2600 · ~1.5% → ~1% leakage
Full wall network complete and operational. Boundary breach now requires extraordinary organised effort detectable before completion. Backup vessel drift approaches near-zero. Enforcement network achieves near-complete coverage. First partial Dyson swarm segments operational by 2600. Charter amendments reflect 350+ years of lived civilizational experience. Informal layer names in common civilizational vocabulary.
5
The Forcefield Century
2600 – 2850 · ~1% → ~0.1% leakage
Dyson swarm energy output reaches levels sufficient for sustained planetary forcefield maintenance. Forcefield prototype tested on a single wall section by 2750. Partial forcefield integration across primary layer boundaries by 2800 — wall breach leakage drops sharply. Full forcefield network operational by 2850. Physical mega-wall and energy barrier now operate simultaneously. Parabolic leakage reduction begins.
6
Mature VMSS
2850 – 3000 · ~0.1% → ~0.01% leakage
Dyson-class energy enables enforcement network densities previously impossible. Backup vessel drift eliminated as a meaningful failure category. Forcefield and physical wall in complete integration — no known bypass mechanism. AI governance systems operating at maximum achievable accuracy after nearly a millennium of refinement. The four major technology convergences — forcefield, energy abundance, backup vessel perfection, AI maturity — compound each other. The last 0.09% of leakage is eliminated faster than the preceding 24.91%.
