Participating in the local economy requires earning local currency through work or receiving it from residents. Punitive reassignment is permanent across all lower layers. Reassignment seals the ceiling, not the floor. STI improvement within a punitive layer affects local standing but does not restore upward eligibility. Only voluntary descenders retain the upward pathway.

VMSS only measures outward actions and execution of harm. Cognition is non-public — the system reads behavioral signals for risk states but never penalises or rewards thoughts, beliefs, or private fantasies. The implant ledger operates on two tracks: the STI for social trust violations, and a criminal record log for hard behavioral flags. The system is physics-like consequence, not ideological judgment.

The economic character follows a deliberate gradient. Upper layers (+1 and Main) are highly socialised — heavy progressive taxation funds the full institutional apparatus. Middle layers (-1 and -2) are hybrid economies where private enterprise coexists with partial institutional presence. -3 is a capitalist frontier — Earth-like capitalism with equity markets, speculation, private banking, and minimal VMSS oversight.

This is intentional design. The population of each layer self-selected or was selected precisely because of their relationship with institutional authority. The economic system matches the demographic and institutional reality of each layer.

Without these, the civilization's core promises fail regardless of progress elsewhere.
