Permanent reassignment protects high-trust environments from repeated exposure to serious violators. VMSS is built around environmental justice rather than repeated reintegration. Once you have demonstrated that you cannot be trusted in a higher ring, the system treats that loss of access as part of the consequence itself. -1 preserves rehabilitation pathways. -2 and -3 are permanent for punitive residents.

VMSS only measures outward actions and execution of harm — never thoughts, beliefs, or private fantasies. The implant ledger operates on two tracks: the STI for social trust violations, and a criminal record log for hard behavioral flags. Neither track monitors thought. Thoughts remain completely private. The system is physics-like consequence, not ideological judgment.

The economic character follows a deliberate gradient. Upper layers (+1 and Main) are highly socialised — heavy progressive taxation funds the full institutional apparatus. Middle layers (-1 and -2) are hybrid economies where private enterprise coexists with partial institutional presence. -3 is a capitalist frontier — Earth-like capitalism with equity markets, speculation, private banking, and minimal VMSS oversight.

This is intentional design. The population of each layer self-selected or was selected precisely because of their relationship with institutional authority. The economic system matches the demographic and institutional reality of each layer.

Selective Ascension Domains — voluntary, revocable purity zones nested within Heaven Layers (+1 Sanctuary). Each is gated by a single measurable metric: zero infidelity, a body composition threshold, a minimum IQ, a wealth floor, and so on. Violation results in automatic exclusion back to the layer below — no VMSS reassignment, only loss of domain access. Citizens may qualify for multiple SADs simultaneously.

Current technology delivers roughly 10% of what VMSS requires — approximately 90% leakage. Three categories are load-bearing: backup vessels (0% delivery — no functional analog exists), the implant ledger (~10%), and autonomous enforcement (~5%). Without these, the civilization's core promises fail regardless of progress elsewhere.

The conceptual and institutional foundations are further along than the headline figure suggests. The blueprint is sound. The enabling technologies are the gap. The 974-year roadmap is built around closing it.
