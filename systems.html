Systems

How doctrine becomes operations. Governance, economy, enforcement, and the principles that hold them together.

Governance

VMSS is governed by a foundational charter developed with AI-assisted modeling. The Chief Architect established the original framework and retains veto authority over changes that contradict its core principles. Long-term decisions are guided by a merit board — high performers across disciplines selected through AI evaluation, not campaigning. Leadership is performance-based: a superior candidate can replace an incumbent by outperforming them.

Policies are stress-tested through simulation before adoption. Some charter provisions may become immutable once they demonstrate extraordinary long-range stability. The charter evolves through supermajority amendment only.

Primary Job Subsidy — The Time Dividend

A supplementary payment matching UBI, available to citizens holding one qualifying job of 20+ hours per week of critical infrastructure work. Its primary purpose is time — 20 qualifying hours unlock the full subsidy, leaving the rest of the week for the citizen's own purposes. It is a labour allocation mechanism, not a moral judgment. Non-qualifying work is not prohibited or stigmatised.

Currency Siloing

+1 Sanctuary and Main Layer share a common currency. -1, -2, and -3 each maintain separate, non-convertible currencies. Wealth accumulated in a lower layer stays there. Wealth from above cannot be transferred down. Visitors to lower layers arrive economically neutral — participation requires earning local currency through work or receiving it from residents.

Savings Circulation Mandate

When population-average savings in Main/Sanctuary crosses $100,000 per citizen, a 10% monthly garnishing activates uniformly — no floor, no exemptions. Self-correcting: at $10,000/month UBI, equilibrium sits at exactly $100k. Scaled equivalents apply in -1 ($50k trigger, 5%) and -2/-3 (UBI-origin savings only). All garnished funds return to the Automation Dividend Treasury as UBI. Each cycle is self-terminating.

Enforcement

Pre-Intervention

Active in +1 Sanctuary and all SADs only. The Threshold Inhibition Protocol halts harmful acts before they complete. Neural inhibition, nano-sedation, and drone countermeasures trigger instantly on detected intent plus imminent execution. No harm completes in Heaven. Perpetrator is reassigned immediately.

Post-Intervention

Active in Main Layer (0) and -1/-2. Harmful acts may complete if warnings are overridden. Victims are restored — murder victims revived via backup vessel, other victims treated via automated therapy. Perpetrators face immediate layer reassignment. The implant record is irrefutable.

Enforcement is not punishment. It is consequence made observable and unavoidable.

Social Trust Index (STI)

A continuous behavioral reliability score maintained for every citizen. Tracks non-criminal conduct: honesty in disputes, contract reliability, harassment patterns, relational breaches. Minor violations remain private. Major violations are logged to a public ledger.

STI does not directly trigger physical enforcement — that distinction is fundamental. It gates access to Trust Purity Domains, SADs, and high-trust opportunities. It makes trust legible rather than assumed. The system operates on two distinct tracks: the STI for social trust violations, and a separate criminal record log for hard behavioral flags. Both travel with every resident across layer boundaries.

Federal Law

VMSS withdraws institutional governance from lower layers. It does not surrender civilizational sovereignty. Federal laws apply across all five rings without exception — their violation produces externalities that cross layer boundaries or threaten the architecture of the civilization itself.

Clean Energy Mandate

Industrial-scale pollution prohibited across all layers. Personal combustion permitted with demonstrable self-cleanup compliance. Industrial operations that cannot meet the standard are prohibited regardless of layer placement.

Nuclear Weapons Prohibition

Absolute across all layers. Manufacture, assembly, or possession triggers immediate reassignment to -3 Terminal. Continued pursuit in -3 is handled by private justice — the population has direct incentive to respond harshly.

Implant & Institutional Hacking

Development or deployment of techniques capable of compromising implants or VMSS institutional systems strictly prohibited. Same consequence pathway as nuclear weapons violation.

Law Enforcement Track

For regulatory violations. Local private enforcement first, escalating through -2, -1, Main Layer, up to +1 Sanctuary for maximum resources. Graduated, proportional, temporary. Upper layer enforcement enters, resolves, and withdraws.

National Defense Track

For organised sovereign threats that exceed law enforcement capacity. Full military deployment — no graduated ladder. Overwhelming by design. Temporary by doctrine. VMSS withdraws when the threat is neutralised.

Military Capability

The military capability of a civilization at VMSS's technological level does not resemble conventional warfare. Two primary instruments are publicly acknowledged. Operational specifics are classified.

Implant Kill Switch

Hardware-level, accessible only through national military command authority. Instantaneous. Simultaneous at any scale. No collateral damage, no emissions. Publicly acknowledged as a deterrent. Activation protocols classified.

Nanobot Neutralisation Plumes

Classified technology for non-implanted threats. Intelligent targeting, near-instant lethality, minimal emissions. Each capsule capable of neutralising thousands. Closes the primary evasion vector — removing an implant to evade the kill switch enters this instrument's envelope instead.
