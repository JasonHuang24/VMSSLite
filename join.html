Ready to Join The Five Rings?

The choice — and the consequences — are yours.

What Joining Means

This is not moving to a better city. It is opting into a different civilizational contract — one where trust, safety, and opportunity are structurally linked to how you actually behave.

You begin in Main Layer (0). From day one: $10,000/month UBI, backup vessel continuity, full access to advanced technologies, and the freedom to build any life you choose. Your outward actions become legible to a system built around consequence. Harm permanently changes the environment you are allowed to inhabit. Sustained compliance opens higher layers. There is no ambiguity about what the system is doing or why.

Join if you want a civilization that takes trust seriously enough to make it architectural. Do not join if you want the benefits without accepting the consequences.

Before You Begin

  1. Read the Charter and Layers — understand the permanent nature of layer reassignment and what each ring actually offers
  2. Consent to the implant procedure — voluntary, but refusal carries real social and access consequences
  3. Submit your application below — reviewed against your Earth history, psychological profile, and behavioral trajectory
